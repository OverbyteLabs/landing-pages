Why async-first companies retain engineers longer
From: your-newsletter@substack.com · Thursday edition

New research from Basecamp and GitLab finds that async-first engineering teams see 34% lower voluntary attrition than their synchronous counterparts over a 24-month window.

The key driver isn't autonomy — it's documentation culture. Engineers in async orgs report clearer decision trails and less time re-litigating past choices in meetings.

The implication for hiring: async fluency is increasingly a differentiator candidates screen for, not just founders.
