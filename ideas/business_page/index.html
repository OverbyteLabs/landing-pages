FAQ

Do you work with early-stage startups? +

Yes — most of our clients are pre-seed to Series A. We're comfortable with ambiguity and tight budgets. Fixed-scope sprints keep risk low for both sides.

What tech stack do you use? +

Primarily Python (FastAPI/Django), TypeScript (Next.js/React), PostgreSQL, and Docker/Kubernetes. We'll adapt to your existing stack where it makes sense.

How quickly can you start? +

Usually within 1–2 weeks of a signed agreement. We keep a small project pipeline so we're not overcommitted.

Do you offer ongoing support after delivery? +

Yes. After a project ships, you can move to a monthly retainer for maintenance, features, and support. Minimum one month, cancel anytime.
