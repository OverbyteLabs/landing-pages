How a streak session works

1

Set your goal

Tell StudyStreak what you're learning and your timeline. It generates a week-by-week roadmap.

2

Study anything

Read a chapter, watch a video, work a problem set. Log what you studied and how long.

3

Coach session

Talk to the Socratic AI. It probes what you read — revealing gaps and cementing what stuck.

4

Reflect and save

Write your reflection. The streak counter ticks. Tomorrow, the breadth feed gives you something new to explore.
